Discover Chile – Travel and Culture Guide

Website Planning Document

Site Name

Discover Chile – Travel and Culture Guide

I chose this name because it clearly represents the purpose of the website. It tells visitors that the site is about Chile, including travel, culture, important destinations, and practical tips for a safe and affordable trip.

Optional domain: discoverchileguide.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide useful information about Chile, including destinations, culture, traditional food, and travel tips. It is designed for people who want to learn about Chile, plan a visit, and find ways to save money during the trip.

Scenarios

  • What are the best places to visit in Chile?
  • What foods and traditions are part of Chilean culture?
  • Which destinations are best for adventure or nature?
  • How can I save money while traveling in Chile?
